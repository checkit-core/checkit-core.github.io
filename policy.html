Overview

“Personal information” is any information you provide to us that personally identifies you, such as your name or e-mail address, or similar information which could be reasonably linked to such identifying information by us. We will only collect, use, and share your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This policy applies whether you use CheckIt Go through https://www.checkit.co.in (the “CheckIt Go website”), our mobile application (the “CheckIt Go” Mobile Application on PlayStore), or any of our other products or services that link to this Privacy Policy (collectively, the “Service”).

What is CheckIt Go?

CheckIt Go is a solution which allows teachers and other professionals to check multiple choice question OMR sheets using a simple android mobile phone. The user uses the mobile CheckIt Go application to click a photograph of the OMR sheet. This photograph is analyzed for each OMR response and compared against the answer key entered by the user. The user is able to instantly see the student’s score on the mobile app. The score and image are stored on the local mobile device for later review.

What type of data is collected?

We collect two types of information about you: (1) Information that you voluntarily provide us by using the CheckIt Go Service (described below) and (2) Information collected automatically as result of your use of the Service. The types and amounts of information collected will vary depending on what features you elect to use. There are several options for further limited the information provided and how that information is shared.

  • Information you provide to CheckIt Go
    Currently the Exam Information and Student Papers Information are stored locally i.e. on the user’s device.
    • Exam Information
      In order to grade/evaluate papers, the teacher must provide information about the exam being graded. This includes the name of the exam, type of exam i.e. Chapter Test, Full course or Quiz, type of key in exam i.e. 20 questions, 40 questions or 100 questions, the answer key, and the marking scheme i.e. the marks offered per question when a question is marked correctly or incorrectly. CheckIt Go will automatically generate the papercode and the date on which the exam is create and store it.
    • Student Papers Information
      While grading papers, the CheckIt Go will capture an image of the paper being graded, the student’s response to each question and the resulting score.
      • Also, CheckIt Go does not store any information about the users which can identify them. However, in near future (within 6 months), it will seek for the following information from the users:
    • CheckIt Go User Account Information
      To create a CheckIt Go user Account, the user must provide an e-mail address that acts as the unique username for their account. They must also provide a password that will be used to login to the CheckIt Go service.
    • Class and Student Information
      The teacher may optionally provide information about their classes and students. A class record consists of the name of the class and the students (below) that are in the class. A student record consists of a first name, last name, a numeric student id, an optional text reference field (labeled External ID), and the classes for this student.
  • Information Collected Automatically
    CheckIt Go, does not log or receive information about the users which can breach their privacy or identify them. The information it receives is through the Google Play dashboard which logs the information like number of installs & uninstalls, android flavor in which the app runs, crash reports. In any case, no user can be identified individually from the information CheckIt Go receives.
